My Musings

I have wanted to produce some sort of content as an outlet for expression for a while now and I figured what better way to do it than through writing. The world is a bit chaotic currently so why not throw what I think into the mix. I tried videos and it did not work too well, so here we are. I will be sharing my opinions and random thoughts, hence the name musings, as well as some recommendations for books, school, and the other stuff that makes up our days. I plan to post weekly, but who knows, maybe more, maybe less. This is partially to open a dialogue about world issues but, I am 18, do not take me too seriously. I am a big proponent of education so that will be the main underlying point I am trying to make. If you decide to read on, I hope you enjoy and possibly take a thing or two away from it.

I am open to discuss any of the topics I post about so feel free to reach out to me on Instagram @mitch_kurtz or by email @ mitchkurtz22@gmail.com.
